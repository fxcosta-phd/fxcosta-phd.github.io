I am a scientist interested in transdisciplinary research, where knowledge is uncovered in multiple scientific fields at the same time, and curiosity driven to develop actionable interventions strategies to real world problems which account for their inherit complexity.

Currently, I work as a postdoctoral researcher at the Complex Adaptive Systems and Computational Intelligence Lab branch in the Católica Biomedical Research Centre from the Universidade Católica Portuguesa. My research focus on understanding patterns of redundancy in biomedical systems from chromossomic interactions to spreading dynamics in social systems.
