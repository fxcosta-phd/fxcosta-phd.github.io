I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the Complex Adaptive Systems and Computational Intelligence lab branch in the Católica Biomedical Research Centre from the Universidade Católica Portuguesa. My research focuses on understanding patterns of redundancy in biomedical systems from chromossomic interactions to spreading dynamics in social systems.

Generally, my interests lie in understanding and developping explainable analysis of real world problems which account for their inherit complexity. Therefore, I see myself as a transdisciplinary scientist for being able to contribute new knowledge within multiple traditional scientific fields.
