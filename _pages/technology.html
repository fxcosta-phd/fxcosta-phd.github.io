Technology

Scientific Freeware

I am part of the research and development team of publicly available software packages to identify important pathways in networked systems and Boolean automata simulations. For more details check them below:

distanceclosure

Finds edges necessary to compute a network closure.

Those edges are also the main ones for dynamics on networks. Check out the details on my thesis.

cubewalkers

A GPU-powered Boolean automata simulator.

CANA

Measures control, canalization and redundancy in Boolean automata.
